Account Deletion Request

Welcome to the Reservice account deletion page. You can request to delete your account and all associated data by following the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion:

  • Send an email to customercare@technde.in from the email address registered with your account.
  • Include your full name, registered email or phone number, and a clear request to delete your account.

Data Deletion Details:

Upon receiving your request, we will:

  • Delete your personal information such as your name, email, phone number, and service booking history.
  • Retain minimal data only if required by law or for legitimate business purposes, and inform you accordingly.
  • Complete the deletion process within 30 days of receiving your request.

If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to contact our support team at customercare@technde.in.
